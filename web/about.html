About Us

Smarasoft Inc was founded in Dallas Texas in 2012. We are focused on creating the latest tools for the wedding and event service industry.

Callseat is our product which will help wedding planners,brides and grooms to simplify the wedding invitation, RSVP and seating process.
Purchase personalized phone numbers from us for your guests to RSVP. Assign seats for your guests. The seating assignment is available to your guests by phone. You can send them emails or text messages with the seating information.
